Frequently Asked Questions about Bet That

Bet That is the first true social peer to peer betting platform, bringing the spirit of the handshake bet to the online space. Type out your own terms and set your own odds on anything verifiable on the internet and post to interest organized communities or send directly to a friend. We’re curating the ultimate social betting platform, where you can win real money, talk trash, and climb social leaderboards on anything from sports to viral internet trends.

Our review system lets us accurately determine the outcome of predictions on anything verifiable on the internet, meaning Bet That users can make predictions on topics ranging from who’s going to be next to leave Love Island to what clothing brand Drake is going to wear in his next Instagram post. Bet That isn’t a sportsbook, we’re a hub for cultural predictions against real people.

We use a combination of AI and community review to make sure every bet is settled fairly and accurately. Our system automatically pulls results from trusted public sources based on the information provided on the bet.

If AI can’t confidently verify the outcome, the bet is sent to community review, where real users are paid to reach a decision. Still not sure the bet was decided fairly? You can appeal to our internal team for a final decision.

This hybrid approach keeps the bet review process for Bet That transparent and fair, even for niche custom bets.

Bet That is built around people, not price charts. While Kalshi and Polymarket operate like financial exchanges where users trade shares in outcomes, Bet That brings back the emotional stakes of a handshake bet against a real person.

Whether you're debating someone in a Bet That community or reading horrible takes about your favorite artist or sports team on X, you can turn that internet discourse into action on Bet That. Set your own terms, challenge anyone, on any platform via link, and put your money where your mouth is.

Bet That Coins are our fun social currency, allowing users to stake their opinion against others for bragging rights, while climbing leaderboards and earning status within the Bet That community. These coins are available to be claimed daily for free and can be used as a free proof point to track the accuracy of your cultural predictions. While Bet That Coins can be accumulated and used within the platform, they have no value outside of the app and cannot be exchanged for external prizes or cash value.

Bet That Cash, on the other hand, is your gateway to our promotional sweepstakes. Bet That Cash is free to claim up to a certain amount or can be purchased and used to play promotional games on the app. Once won through gameplay, Bet That Cash can be used to play more promotional games or used to earn prizes once you meet the minimum play and complete our verification process as outlined in our Terms of Use.

Bet That Cash can be obtained for free with select Bet That Coin purchases or through our alternative entry options. NO PURCHASE IS NECESSARY to acquire Bet That Cash. For full participation details, please see our Sweepstakes Rules.
