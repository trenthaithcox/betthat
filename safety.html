Safety & Community Guidelines

At Bet That, we're committed to creating a fun, respectful, and safe environment for everyone.

Our Content Principles

  • No predictions that involve harm to others
  • No predictions involving illegal activities
  • No hate speech, discrimination, or bullying
  • No sharing of personal information without consent

Moderation & Reporting

  • Easy in-app reporting of inappropriate content
  • Automated content filtering system
  • Human moderation team that reviews flagged content
  • Ability to block users and control visibility

Privacy Protection

  • Control over prediction visibility (public/private)
  • Option to use a username rather than real name
  • No sharing your data with third parties
  • Transparent privacy policy on data usage

Responsible Use

  • Keep predictions lighthearted and friendly
  • Be gracious whether right or wrong
  • Respect different opinions and perspectives
  • Be mindful of how much time you spend on the app
